# How Long Tho

You're not lazy. You're time-blind.

You care. You try. And you're still late. Not because you're lazy — because your
brain doesn't feel time passing. How Long Tho makes time visible, so it stops
catching you off guard.

[Try the demo](https://app.howlongtho.com) · [iOS App Store](#) · [Google Play](#) · [Get notified](#mailing-list)

## What it is

If you've ever been called lazy, flaky, or irresponsible when you were genuinely
trying — this app is for you.

Time blindness is real. Your brain doesn't register time the way others' do.
That's not a character flaw. It's a wiring difference — and it deserves a tool,
not a lecture.

How Long Tho gives you an external sense of time, so you feel deadlines coming
instead of finding out too late.

- A countdown you can see — not a date buried in a calendar
- A hard alarm that cuts through hyperfocus when nothing else can
- A separate "time to leave" timer, so you're not just on time for the event —
  you're on time to go
- Every timer quietly trains your sense of how long things actually take
