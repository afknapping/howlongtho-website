# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 12, 2026*

How Long Tho is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains,
in plain language, what we do and don't do with your data.

## What we don't do

- We don't have a server. There's no backend, no account system, no login.
- We don't collect analytics or usage data.
- We don't include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
- We never sell or share your data — because we don't have any of it to sell.

## What the app accesses, and why

- **Calendar and Reminders** (via Apple's EventKit) — read on your device only,
  so you can start a countdown from an upcoming event or reminder. Never
  transmitted anywhere, including to us.
- **Alarms and notifications** (via Apple's AlarmKit and UserNotifications) —
  used to schedule the countdown alerts and daily reminder you set. Stay
  entirely within iOS.
- **Live Activities** (via Apple's ActivityKit) — powers the Lock Screen and
  Dynamic Island countdown card. Local to your device.

## iCloud sync

Your app settings (like your daily reminder time or sound preferences) sync
across your own devices using Apple's iCloud key-value storage. This data
lives in your personal iCloud account, governed by
[Apple's own privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) — we never
see it.

## Diagnostics

If you've opted in to "Share With App Developers" in iOS Settings, Apple may
share anonymized crash reports with us to help fix bugs. This is controlled
entirely by your iOS settings, not by anything in the app itself, and we
can't identify you from it.

## Children's privacy

How Long Tho is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly
collect information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If anything here changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date
above.
